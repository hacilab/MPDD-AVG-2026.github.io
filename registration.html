Registration

The MPDD-AVG Challenge is open to any researcher from any organization worldwide. To participate, please complete both steps below.

1

Download and sign the End-User License Agreement (EULA)

Download the MPDD-AVG Dataset License Agreement (PDF) and sign it at your end. The agreement must be signed by a full-time faculty member or researcher.

Please email the signed EULA to sstcneu@163.com (CC: fuchangzeng@qhd.neu.edu.cn | shangming@mails.neu.edu.cn | zhangyiming1@mails.neu.edu.cn). Include your team name in the email — this name will be used as your CodaLab group name for the leaderboard.

Applications not following the EULA signing process may be ignored.

2

Complete the online registration form

Fill in the registration form below with your team information, institution details, and track selection. You will receive a confirmation email after submission.

Challenge Rules

✓ Allowed: Pre-trained weights from open-source datasets or models
✗ Prohibited: Private or in-house datasets for pre-training
✓ Limit: Maximum 5 submission attempts per sub-track per day
✓ Required: Full reproducibility — code, models, and checkpoints
✓ Evaluation: Accuracy, Macro-F1, CCC, Cohen's Kappa
✓ Eligibility: Open to all researchers worldwide
